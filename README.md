# Superstore Financial Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## üìå Project Overview
This project analyzes the financial and operational performance of a retail business using **Power BI**.
The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into **clear, interactive, and actionable insights**
to support data-driven decision-making by management and business stakeholders.

The project focuses on understanding **revenue generation, profitability, order behavior, and cost efficiency**
across regions, customer segments, and product categories.

## üõ† Tools & Skills Used
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX (CALCULATE, RANKX, TOPN, Measures)
- Data Modeling
- KPI Development
- Data Visualization & Dashboard Design
- Business Analytics & Storytelling

---

## üß† Business Questions Answered

### üîπ Revenue Performance
- Which regions contribute the most and least to total revenue?
- How does revenue vary across customer segments (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)?
- Which product categories and sub-categories are the primary revenue drivers?
- Which states and cities generate the highest revenue contribution?
- How evenly distributed is revenue across regions and product lines?

---

### üîπ Profitability Analysis
- Which regions are the most profitable after accounting for costs?
- How does profit differ across customer segments and product categories?
- Which cities generate high revenue but low profit, indicating margin pressure?
- What is the overall profit margin, and how does it vary by region?
- How does profitability trend on a monthly basis?

---

### üîπ Order Behavior & Fulfillment
- How many orders are processed within the selected period?
- What is the average number of items per order?
- How do orders vary by product category and customer segment?
- Which shipping modes are most frequently used by customers?
- Does order volume align with profitability across regions and segments?

---

### üîπ Cost Efficiency & Control
- What is the total operational cost incurred?
- What is the average cost per order?
- What is the average cost per item sold?
- How does cost compare to revenue across product categories?
- What percentage of revenue is consumed by operational costs?
- Where do cost inefficiencies negatively impact profitability?

---

### üîπ Strategic Performance Insights
- Which combinations of region, category, and segment drive the strongest performance?
- Where should management focus to improve margins without sacrificing revenue?
- Which markets or product lines show potential for optimization or expansion?
- How effectively is the business converting orders into profitable outcomes?
